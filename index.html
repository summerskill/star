Welcome to Star Lesson Plans

The material on the website includes example lesson plans to aid in preparation and instruction. The material provided is included to link to previous knowledge of the student and arouse curiosity.

The site provides visual aids, worksheets, and links for additional materials as suggestions for required student participation for association and comparison in Geography. Exercises include venues for students to apply reflection and experience. And on completion apply learned principles to new situations.


Vision

Each student’s success starts in primary and secondary settings with child readiness to learn; cognitively, physically, socially and emotionally. As a group, we can together, work to improve learning environments and learning outcomes in our nations economically challenged areas. Each child’s future learning successes depend on a strong reading foundation as an essential building block. Providing each student, a foundation for future learning success outcomes.

We aspire to create learning environments that motivate children to reach for knowledge and adopt enthusiasm for learning. Enthusiasm for learning is a driving force toward post-secondary education. Our youth have the absolute right to gain equal education to prepare them with the right skills sets and tools to progress to post-secondary education. With these acquired skill sets and tools, allowing them to gain their desired education and training needed to successfully enter the workforce.
